## Election Flood Risk
- GE15 (19 Nov 2022) was held during NE monsoon → Baram polling STATIONS WERE FLOODED, delayed 2 days
- Dec-Jan is the HIGHEST risk period - avoid for election
- May-Sep is the SAFEST window
