This is the official list of '''candidates for the [[2022 Malaysian general election]],''' which was published on 5 November 2022 by the [[Election Commission of Malaysia|Election Commission of Malaysia (SPR)]].<ref>{{Cite web |title= |url=https://www.spr.gov.my/sites/default/files/KM%20BIL%2028%202022%20%20MESYUARAT%20KHAS%20PRU%20%20KE%2015.pdf}}</ref>

This election saw a record 945 candidates contesting in all 222 parliamentary seats nationwide, among them a record 108 independent candidates.<ref>{{Cite web |title=GE15 in numbers |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/11/06/ge15-in-numbers |access-date=2022-11-06 |website=The Star |date=6 November 2022 |language=en}}</ref>
