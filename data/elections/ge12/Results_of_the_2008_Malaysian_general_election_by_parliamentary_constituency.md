[[File:Dewan Rakyat 2008 Equal Area.svg|thumb|400px|Dewan Rakyat results of the 2013 Malaysian General Election (equal-area representation)]]
These are the '''election results of the [[2008 Malaysian general election]] by parliamentary constituency'''. These [[Members of the Dewan Rakyat, 12th Malaysian Parliament|members of parliament]] (MPs) representing their constituency from the first sitting of 12th Malaysian Parliament to its dissolution.

The parliamentary election deposit was set at [[Malaysian ringgit|RM]] 10,000 per candidate. Similar to previous elections, the election deposit will be forfeited if the particular candidate had failed to secure at least 12.5% or one-eighth of the votes.
