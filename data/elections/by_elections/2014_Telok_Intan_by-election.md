}}A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] parliamentary seat of '''[[Teluk Intan (federal constituency)|Teluk Intan]]''' on 31 May 2014 following the nomination day on 19 May 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fz.com/content/teluk-intan-election-polling-may-31-nomination-may-19|title=Teluk Intan by-election: Polling May 31, nomination May 19 &#124|publisher=FZ|accessdate=8 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924020429/http://www.fz.com/content/teluk-intan-election-polling-may-31-nomination-may-19|archive-date=24 September 2015|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2014/05/08/may-31-set-teluk-intan-election/ |title=May 31 set for Teluk Intan by-election |publisher=The Rakyat Post |accessdate=8 May 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140508223131/http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2014/05/08/may-31-set-teluk-intan-election/ |archive-date=8 May 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The seat was vacated after the death of the incumbent MP, [[Seah Leong Peng]] from bladder cancer in [[Kuala Lumpur]] on 1 May 2014.<ref name="TMI">{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=DAP lawmaker dies of cancer, says paper|date=1 May 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/dap-lawmaker-dies-of-cancer-says-paper|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140504083723/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/dap-lawmaker-dies-of-cancer-says-paper|archive-date=4 May 2014|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref> Seah was a lawmaker from the [[Democratic Action Party]] (DAP), a component party of [[Pakatan Rakyat]] (PR) coalition.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/teluk-intan-mps-death-puts-another-poll-on-daps-plate |title=Teluk Intan MP's death puts another poll on DAP's plate |publisher=The Malay Mail Online |date=2 May 2014 |accessdate=8 May 2014}}</ref> In the [[2013 Malaysian general election|2013 general election]], he defeated [[Barisan Nasional]](BN) candidate and [[Parti Gerakan Rakyat Malaysia|Gerakan]] president [[Mah Siew Keong]] and an independent candidate by 7,313 votes.<ref name="TMI" /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://undi.info/perak/p/P76 |title=P76 Teluk Intan |publisher=undi.info |access-date=2014-05-05 |archive-date=2014-05-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140506014458/http://undi.info/perak/p/P76 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.malaysia-chronicle.com/index.php?option=com_k2&view=item&id=273781:daps-teluk-intan-mp-dies-of-cancer-at-age-48&Itemid=2#.U2nlMlcVeDg |title=DAP's Teluk Intan MP dies of cancer at age 48 |publisher=Malaysian Chronicle |date=1 May 2014 }}</ref>

The PR candidate in the by-election was DAP's [[Dyana Sofya Mohd Daud]], notable for her status as a rare  [[Malay People|Malay]] politician in the DAP.<ref>{{cite web|author=Eileen Ng|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/dap-names-dyana-sofya-as-teluk-intan-candidate|title=DAP names Dyana Sofya as Teluk Intan candidate|publisher=The Malaysian Insider|date=17 May 2014|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141031090519/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/dap-names-dyana-sofya-as-teluk-intan-candidate|archive-date=31 October 2014}}</ref> On 17 May 2014, BN announced that it would again field Mah Siew Keong.<ref>{{cite web|author=Audrey Dermawan|url=http://www.nst.com.my/latest/font-color-red-teluk-intan-font-bn-names-mah-siew-keong-as-its-candidate-1.599511|title=Teluk Intan: BN names Mah Siew Keong as its candidate|publisher=New Straits Times|date=17 May 2014|accessdate=18 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140518005912/http://www.nst.com.my/latest/font-color-red-teluk-intan-font-bn-names-mah-siew-keong-as-its-candidate-1.599511|archive-date=18 May 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> 60,349 voters were eligible to vote in the by-election.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/gerakans-mah-takes-on-dyana-sofya-in-teluk-intan-by-election |title=Gerakan's Mah takes on Dyana Sofya in Teluk Intan by-election |publisher=The Malaysian Insider |date=19 May 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303224936/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/gerakans-mah-takes-on-dyana-sofya-in-teluk-intan-by-election |archive-date=3 March 2016 |url-status=dead |df=dmy-all }}</ref>

== Results ==
