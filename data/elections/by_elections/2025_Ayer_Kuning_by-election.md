The '''2025 Ayer Kuning by-election''' was a by-election for the [[Perak State Legislative Assembly]] seat of '''[[Ayer Kuning (state constituency)|Ayer Kuning]]''' held on 26 April 2025. It was called following the death of incumbent, [[Ishsam Shahruddin]] on 22 February 2025.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning assemblyman dies after collapsing during football match in Penang|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2025/02/1178834/ayer-kuning-assemblyman-dies-after-collapsing-during-football-match|date=22 February 2025|access-date=22 February 2025|website=[[New Straits Times]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning assemblyman Ishsam Shahruddin dies after collapsing during football event in Penang|url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2025/02/22/ayer-kuning-assemblyman-ishsam-shahruddin-dies-after-collapsing-during-football-event-in-penang/167625|date=22 February 2025|access-date=22 February 2025|website=[[Malay Mail]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=ADUN Ayer Kuning meninggal dunia|url=https://www.sinarharian.com.my/article/714173/berita/semasa/adun-ayer-kuning-meninggal-dunia|date=22 February 2025|access-date=22 February 2025|website=[[Sinar Harian]] |language=ms}}</ref> Ishsam served as Member of the [[Perak State Legislative Assembly]] (MLA) from 2022.

[[Barisan Nasional]]'s [[Mohamad Yusri Bakir]] defeated both [[Perikatan Nasional]]'s Abdul Muhaimin Malek and the [[Socialist Party of Malaysia]]'s Bawani Kaniapan by a majority of  5,006 votes.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning by-election: BN wins with larger majority|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2025/04/1207873/ayer-kuning-election-bn-wins-larger-majority|date=26 April 2025|access-date=26 April 2025|website=[[New Straits Times]]|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=BN retains Ayer Kuning with bigger majority|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2025/04/26/bn-retains-ayer-kuning-with-bigger-majority|date=26 April 2025|access-date=26 April 2025|website=[[Free Malaysia Today]] |language=en}}</ref>

== Nomination ==
On 24 February, the [[National Trust Party (Malaysia)|National Trust Party]] (AMANAH), who previously contested the seat under [[Pakatan Harapan]]'s (PH) banner in the last election, confirmed it would make way for the [[Barisan Nasional]] (BN) candidate in the by-election.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning by-election: Amanah to throw its support behind Barisan candidate|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2025/02/24/ayer-kuning-by-election-amanah-to-throw-its-support-behind-barisan-candidate|date=24 February 2025|access-date=25 February 2025|website=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|language=en}}</ref> BN Chairman [[Ahmad Zahid Hamidi]] said the candidate will be selected among six shortlisted names chosen by Tapah UMNO, and is expected to be announced a week before nomination day.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning polls: BN to reveal candidate a week before nomination day|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2025/03/10/ayer-kuning-polls-bn-to-reveal-candidate-a-week-before-nomination-day|date=10 March 2025|access-date=13 March 2025|website=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|language=en}}</ref> On 7 April, BN named Islamic academic and Tapah UMNO Secretary [[Mohamad Yusri Bakir]] as its candidate.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Mohamad Yusri named BN candidate for Ayer Kuning by-election|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2025/04/1198534/mohamad-yusri-named-bn-candidate-ayer-kuning-election|date=7 April 2025|access-date=7 April 2025|website=[[New Straits Times]]|language=en}}</ref>

On 28 February, [[Perikatan Nasional]] (PN) Chairman [[Muhyiddin Yassin]] confirmed that the coalition's candidate will be from the [[Malaysian Islamic Party]] (PAS).<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ayer Kuning by-election: Perikatan to field PAS candidate, says Muhyiddin|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2025/02/28/ayer-kuning-by-election-perikatan-to-field-pas-candidate-says-muhyiddin|date=28 February 2025|access-date=7 March 2025|website=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|language=en}}</ref> PAS' Tapah branch was also reported to have shortlisted three names as possible candidates, primarily young local candidates.<ref>{{Cite web|title=PRK N.48 Ayer Kuning: PAS Tapah kemuka tiga calon|url=https://harakahdaily.net/index.php/2025/03/02/prk-n48-ayer-kuning-pas-tapah-kemuka-tiga-calon/|date=2 March 2025|access-date=7 March 2025|website=[[Harakah (newspaper)|Harakah]]|language=ms}}</ref> On 8 April, PN announced Tapah PN chairman Abdul Muhaimin Malek as its candidate.<ref>{{Cite web|title=PN names Muhaimin Malek for Ayer Kuning polls|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2025/04/08/pn-names-muhaimin-malek-for-ayer-kuning-polls/|date=8 April 2025|access-date=8 April 2025|website=[[Free Malaysia Today]]|language=en}}</ref> 

The [[Socialist Party of Malaysia]] (PSM), who also contested the seat in the last election, was reported to be in consideration on whether to contest in the by-election. PSM Chairman [[Michael Jeyakumar Devaraj]] said the matter would be discussed among the party, and with electoral allies the [[Malaysian United Democratic Alliance]] (MUDA) and [[Parti Rakyat Malaysia]] (PRM).<ref>{{Cite web|title=PSM idam kerusi DUN Ayer Kuning|url=https://www.sinarharian.com.my/article/716016/berita/politik/psm-idam-kerusi-dun-ayer-kuning|date=5 March 2025|access-date=7 March 2025|website=[[Sinar Harian]]|language=ms}}</ref> On 22 March, PSM confirmed it will contest in the by-election, with MUDA pledging to support their bid the following day.<ref>{{Cite web|title=PSM to contest in Ayer Kuning by-election|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2025/03/22/psm-to-contest-in-ayer-kuning-by-election|date=22 March 2025|access-date=22 March 2025|website=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Muda to back PSM candidate in Ayer Kuning by-election|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2025/03/23/muda-to-back-psm-candidate-in-ayer-kuning-by-election/|date=23 March 2025|access-date=23 March 2025|website=[[Free Malaysia Today]]|language=en}}</ref> On 8 April, PSM once again fielded Bawani Kaniapan as their candidate for the seat.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bawani calon PSM kembali bertarung di Ayer Kuning|url=https://m.malaysiakini.com/news/739469|date=8 April 2025|access-date=8 April 2025|website=[[Malaysiakini]]|language=ms}}</ref>
