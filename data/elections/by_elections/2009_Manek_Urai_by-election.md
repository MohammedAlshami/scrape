The '''2009 Manek Urai by-election''' is a by-election for the [[Kelantan State Legislative Assembly]] state seat of '''[[Manek Urai]]''', [[Malaysia]] that were held on 14 July 2009. It was called following the death of the incumbent, [[Ismail Yaacob]] on 22 May 2009.

== Background ==
Ismail Yaacob, from [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party]] (PAS), were elected to the Kelantan State Legislative Assembly state seat of Manek Urai at the 1986 Kelantan state election, winning the seat from [[Barisan Nasional]] (BN). He has since held the seat for 4 terms, but not defending the seat in 2004 which saw the seat fell back to BN's hand. Ismail returned to contest the seat in 2008 state election, winning back the seat from BN. He was the deputy chief of the party's Kuala Krai division.<ref>{{cite web |title=Five-term PAS state rep in Kelantan suffers heart attack while ill in hospital |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2009/05/23/fiveterm-pas-state-rep-in-kelantan-suffers-heart-attack-while-ill-in-hospital/ |website=The Star |access-date=7 March 2025 |language=en |date=23 May 2009}}</ref>

On 22 May 2009, Ismail died at Raja Zainab Hospital, Kota Bharu, after being admitted to the hospital since 26 April 2009 for treatment after suffering a heart attack.<ref>{{cite web |title=Kelantan PAS state rep dies |url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/104855 |website=Malaysiakini |access-date=7 March 2025 |date=22 May 2009|url-access=subscription}}</ref> His death means that Manek Urai state seat were vacated. This necessitates for by-election to be held, as the seat were vacated more that 2 years before the expiry of Kelantan assembly current term. Election Commission of Malaysia (SPR) announced on 28 May 2009 that the by-election for the seat will be held on 14 July 2009, with 6 July 2009 set as the nomination day.<ref>{{cite web |title=P'raya DUN Manek Urai 14 Jul |url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/105273 |website=Malaysiakini |access-date=7 March 2025 |language=ms |date=28 May 2009}}</ref>

== Nomination and campaign ==
After nomination closed, it was confirmed that BN will face PAS in a straight fight for the Manek Urai seat.<ref>{{cite web |title=Sah! Hanya satu lawan satu di Manek Urai - Semasa {{!}} mStar |url=https://www.mstar.com.my/lokal/semasa/2009/07/06/sah-hanya-satu-lawan-satu-di-manek-urai |website=www.mstar.com.my |access-date=7 March 2025 |language=ms |date=6 July 2009}}</ref> BN nominated Tuan Aziz Tuan Mat @ Tuan Hamat from [[United Malays National Organization]] (UMNO), a manager at the Southern Kelantan Development Board (KESEDAR) and the party's Kuala Krai youth chief.<ref>{{cite web |last1=AZHAR |first1=SYED |title=Kuala Krai Umno youth chief is BN’s man for Manek Urai |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2009/06/28/kuala-krai-umno-youth-chief-is-bns-man-for-manek-urai |website=The Star |access-date=9 March 2025 |language=en |date=28 June 2009}}</ref> PAS meanwhile nominated Mohd Fauzi Abdullah, a fish wholesaler, and treasurer for the party's Kuala Krai division.<ref>{{cite web |title=Tuduhan rasuah asam garam politik - Tuan Aziz Tuan Mat - Semasa {{!}} mStar |url=https://www.mstar.com.my/lokal/semasa/2009/06/30/tuduhan-rasuah-asam-garam-politik--tuan-aziz-tuan-mat |website=www.mstar.com.my |access-date=7 March 2025 |language=ms |date=30 June 2009}}</ref> Parti Angkatan Keadilan Insan Malaysia (AKIM) president Hanafi Mamat, who was said to be contesting in the by-election, did not turn up for the nomination process.<ref>{{cite web |author1=[[Bernama]] |title=Straight fight in Manek Urai |url=https://bersih.org/2009/07/06/straight-fight-in-manek-urai/ |website=bersih.org |access-date=7 March 2025 |date=6 July 2009}}</ref>
