A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Kedah State Legislative Assembly|Kedah State Assembly]] seat of '''[[Lunas, Kedah|Lunas]]''' on 29 November 2000 following the nomination day on 21 November 2000.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Tranung Kite  Cyberlink|accessdate=28 February 2017|title=Pilihanraya Kecil DUN Lunas - BA Mesti Menang|url=http://www.tranungkite.net/lama/berita11/habibu12.htm}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after the incumbent assemblyman, Joe Fernandez, the deputy state chief of the [[Malaysian Indian Congress]] (MIC), of the ruling [[Barisan Nasional]] coalition, was assassinated in [[Bukit Mertajam]] on 4 November 2000.<ref>{{cite news|work=Yokesun Ching|access-date=28 February 2017|title=Assassination Puts Malaysian Politicians on Alert|date=30 November 2000|url=http://www.oocities.org/yoke_sun/0012/MA-assass.html}}</ref> He won a 4,700-vote majority by polling 9,760 votes against three candidates: 5,060 votes for M. Kathiravelo of [[Democratic Action Party (Malaysia)|Democratic Action Party]] (DAP), Ibrahim Mohamed Rashdi (Independent) 2,158 votes and N. Letchumanan (Independent) 87 votes in the [[1999 Malaysian general election|general elections in 1999]].

S. Anthonysamy of MIC was chosen to be the Barisan Nasional candidate for the by-election, while the opposition [[Barisan Alternatif]] coalition chose [[Saifuddin Nasution Ismail]] of [[Parti Keadilan Nasional]] (KeADILan) as their candidate. The by-election was a three-corner between two of them and independent N. Letchumanan.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[Utusan Malaysia]]|access-date=28 February 2017|title=Lunas by-election a three-cornered fight|date=21  November 2000|url=http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2000&dt=1121&pub=Utusan_Express&sec=Latest_News&pg=lt_01.htm|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170301005653/http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2000&dt=1121&pub=Utusan_Express&sec=Latest_News&pg=lt_01.htm|archive-date=1 March 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

The by-election was won by Saifuddin Nasution Ismail with a majority of 530 votes, polling 10,511 votes.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/1046821.stm|title=Mahathir's coalition loses by-election|date=29 November 2000|publisher=[[BBC News]]|accessdate=28 May 2010}}</ref> A 77.58 percentage turnout of a total 26,746 registered voters came out to vote.<ref name=lunas>{{cite news |url=http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2000&dt=1130&pub=utusan_malaysia&sec=Muka_Hadapan&pg=fp_05.htm |title=Keadilan menang pilihan raya kecil Lunas |date=30 November 2000 |author=OTHMAN MOHAMAD |publisher=[[Utusan Malaysia]] |access-date=28 February 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170228164613/http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2000&dt=1130&pub=utusan_malaysia&sec=Muka_Hadapan&pg=fp_05.htm |archive-date=28 February 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
