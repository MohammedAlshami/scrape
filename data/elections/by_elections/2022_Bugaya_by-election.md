The '''2022 Bugaya by-election''' was a by-election held on 19 November 2022 for the [[Sabah State Legislative Assembly]] seat of [[Bugaya (state constituency)|Bugaya]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2022/10/07/king-revokes-emergency-proclamations-for-gerik-batu-sapi-and-bugaya/|title=King revokes emergency proclamations for Gerik, Batu Sapi and Bugaya|website=Free Malaysia Today|date=7 October 2022|accessdate=17 October 2022}}</ref> It was called following the death of the incumbent, [[Manis Muka Mohd Darah]] on 17 November 2020.<ref name="Manis Muka died">{{cite news|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2020/11/641829/bugaya-assemblyman-manis-muka-darah-dies|title=Bugaya assemblyman Manis Muka Darah dies|date=17 November 2020|accessdate=17 October 2022|author=Abdul Rahemang Taming|publisher=New Straits Times}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.utusan.com.my/berita/2020/11/adun-bugaya-meninggal-dunia/|title=ADUN Bugaya Manis Muka meninggal dunia|author=Mohd Fadhli Mohd Sulaiman|work=[[Utusan Malaysia]]|language=ms|date=17 November 2020|accessdate=17 October 2022}}</ref> Manis Muka was a 2-term state assemblywoman first elected at the [[2018 Sabah state election]]. She was a member of the [[Sabah Heritage Party]] (WARISAN).

The by-election was to be held within 60 days after the seat was declared vacant, however due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Sabah]], then Prime Minister [[Muhyiddin Yassin]] advised the [[Yang di-Pertuan Agong]] to declare an emergency to postpone the by-election.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.malaysiakini.com/news/555472|title=Localised emergencies declared in Gerik and Bugaya, by-elections on hold|website=Malaysiakini|date=16 December 2020|accessdate=17 October 2022}}</ref> The emergency proclamation was eventually revoked on 7 October 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/emergency-ordinances-proclamations-prevented-byelections-batu-sapi-gerik-bugaya-revoked|title=Emergency ordinances, proclamations that prevented by-elections in Batu Sapi, Gerik, Bugaya revoked|website=The Edge (Malaysia)|date=7 October 2022|accessdate=17 October 2022|archive-date=18 October 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221018233743/https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/emergency-ordinances-proclamations-prevented-byelections-batu-sapi-gerik-bugaya-revoked|url-status=dead}}</ref> The by-election was held concurrently with the [[2022 Malaysian general election]] and the [[2022 Malaysian state elections]] for the state assemblies of [[Pahang State Legislative Assembly|Pahang]], [[Perak State Legislative Assembly|Perak]] and [[Perlis State Legislative Assembly|Perlis]].

==Nominations==
Candidates for the Bugaya by-election has been set by Election Commission (EC) to be on 5 November, the same as the general election and the state election for Perak, Perlis and Pahang.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Alhadjri |first1=Alyaa |title=Malaysia goes to the polls on Nov 19 |url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/640363 |website=Malaysiakini |access-date=10 September 2023 |date=20 October 2022}}</ref> After the nomination closed, 7 candidates were approved by EC: Jamil Hamzah from WARISAN, Abdul Aziz Mohd Ibno from [[Barisan Nasional]] (BN), Zulfadzli Shi Ahmad as independent, Karil Kuraini from [[Parti Perpaduan Rakyat Sabah]] (PPRS), Arastam Pandorog from [[Pakatan Harapan]] (PH), Hassan Abu Bakar from [[Homeland Fighter's Party]] (PEJUANG), and Nazmawati Walli from [[Parti Bangsa Malaysia]] (PBM).<ref>{{cite web |author1=[[Bernama]] |title=7-cornered contest in Bugaya by-election |url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2022/11/05/7-cornered-contest-in-bugaya-by-election/ |website=Free Malaysia Today |access-date=10 September 2023 |date=5 November 2022}}</ref>

Jamil, a syariah lawyer by trade, is the WARISAN Central Youth Exco member, and the party's Semporna branch committee member.<ref>{{cite web |title=JAMIL HAMZAH |url=https://pru.sinarharian.com.my/calon/7818/jamil-hamzah |website=PRU @ Sinar Harian |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=MS}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author1=Durie Rainer Fong |title=Warisan's Jamil Hamzah sworn in as new Bugaya assemblyman |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/11/25/warisan039s-jamil-hamzah-sworn-in-as-new-bugaya-assemblyman |website=The Star |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=en}}</ref> He is also the nephew of the party leader [[Shafie Apdal]], who announced Jamil's candidacy on 3 November.<ref name='BugayaTolakWarisan'>{{cite web |last1=SULAIMAN |first1=MOHD FADHLI MOHD |title=Pengundi Bugaya tolak calon parti Warisan? |url=https://www.utusan.com.my/nasional/2022/11/pengundi-bugaya-tolak-calon-parti-warisan/ |website=Utusan Malaysia |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=ms-MY |date=10 November 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author1=STEPHANIE LEE |author2=DURIE RAINER FONG |title=PRU15: Adik Shafie Apdal antara muka baharu Warisan di Sabah |url=https://majoriti.com.my/berita/2022/11/03/pru15-adik-shafie-apdal-antara-muka-baharu-warisan-di-sabah |website=The Star via Majoriti.com.my |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=ms |date=3 November 2022}}</ref>

Abdul Aziz were announced as BN candidate on 2 November.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Muhamad |first1=Hasimi |title=PRU15: Abdul Aziz Mohd Ibno calon BN di DUN Bugaya |url=https://www.astroawani.com/berita-politik/pru15-abdul-aziz-mohd-ibno-calon-bn-di-dun-bugaya-389130 |website=www.astroawani.com |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=MS |date=2 November 2022}}</ref> A former teacher, Abdul Aziz is the [[United Malays National Organization|UMNO]] deputy chief for its Semporna branch.<ref name="AzizTekadRampasBugaya">{{cite web |last1=SULAIMAN |first1=MOHD FADHLI MOHD |title=PRK DUN Bugaya: Abdul Aziz tekad rampas kerusi milik Warisan |url=https://www.utusan.com.my/berita/2022/11/prk-dun-bugaya-abdul-aziz-tekad-rampas-kerusi-milik-warisan/ |website=Utusan Malaysia |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=ms-MY |date=16 November 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=ABD AZIZ MOHD IBNO |url=https://pru.sinarharian.com.my/calon/7821/abd-aziz-mohd-ibno |website=PRU @ Sinar Harian |access-date=12 September 2023 |language=MS}}</ref> He has said that improving the basic infrastructure in the constituency would be his focus if he is elected.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Deasoka |first1=Editorial |title=Selesai masalah infra jadi fokus calon BN DUN Bugaya |url=https://deasoka.com/2022/selesai-masalah-infra-jadi-fokus-calon-bn-dun-bugaya/semasa/ |website=DEASOKA |access-date=10 September 2023 |language=MS |date=12 November 2022}}</ref> He also determined to resume projects under BN that were stopped after its defeat in the [[2018 Sabah state election]], such as building a hospital in Semporna.<ref name="AzizTekadRampasBugaya"/>

==Aftermath==
Jamil, the winner in the by-election, were sworn in as the new MLA for Bugaya in front of the Sabah Assembly Speaker on 25 November, at the start of the 3rd Session of the 16th Sabah Assembly sitting.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Axtius |first1=Mirlen |title=Jamil Hamzah angkat sumpah sebagai ADUN Bugaya baharu |url=https://www.astroawani.com/berita-politik/jamil-hamzah-angkat-sumpah-sebagai-adun-bugaya-baharu-393662 |website=www.astroawani.com |access-date=10 September 2023 |date=25 November 2022}}</ref>
