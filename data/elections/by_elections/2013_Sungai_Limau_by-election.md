A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Kedah State Legislative Assembly|Kedah State Assembly]] seat of '''[[Sungai Limau]]''' on 4 November 2013 with nomination day on 23 October 2013.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Sungai Limau by-election on November 4|date=3 October 2013|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-by-election-on-november-4|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005012752/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-by-election-on-november-4|archive-date=5 October 2013|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref> The seat was vacated after the death of the incumbent five-term assemblyman and former [[List of Menteris Besar of Kedah|Menteri Besar of Kedah]], [[Azizan Abdul Razak]] on 26 September 2013 from heart complications.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Former Kedah MB Azizan dies|date=26 September 2013|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/former-kedah-mb-azizan-dies|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005002451/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/former-kedah-mb-azizan-dies|archive-date=5 October 2013|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref> Tan Sri Azizan was an assemblyman from the [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party]] and the first PAS Menteri Besar of Kedah.

On 21 October, Barisan Nasional made announcement that Dr Ahmad Sohaimi Lazim, lecturer in [[Sultan Idris University of Education]] has been named as BN candidate <ref>{{cite news|work=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|title=Lecturer named BN candidate for Sungai Limau by-election|date=21 October 2013|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/News/Nation/2013/10/21/Sungai-Limau-BN-candidate.aspx|access-date=21 October 2013|archive-date=24 October 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131024010313/http://www.thestar.com.my/News/Nation/2013/10/21/Sungai-Limau-BN-candidate.aspx|url-status=dead}}</ref> while PAS named Jerai PAS Youth chief Mohd Azam Samad as PAS candidate.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=ABN News|title=PAS names Jerai youth chief as Sg. Limau by-election candidate|date=21 October 2013|url=http://news.abnxcess.com/2013/10/pas-names-jerai-youth-chief-as-sg-limau-by-election-candidate/}}</ref>  The election was seen as a sort of referendum on current BN Menteri Besar of Kedah, [[Mukhriz Mahathir]] who failed in his bid to be elected as UMNO vice-president in the UMNO polls on 19 October 2013.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malay Mail]]|title=Test for Mukhriz as Sungai Limau heads to polls|date=4 November 2013|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/test-for-mukhriz-as-sungai-limau-heads-to-polls}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Sungai Limau polls a baptism of fire for Mukhriz Mahathir|date=4 November 2013|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-polls-a-baptism-of-fire-for-mukhriz-mahathir|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131104212943/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-polls-a-baptism-of-fire-for-mukhriz-mahathir|archive-date=4 November 2013|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Sungai Limau a litmus test for both PAS and Umno|date=23 October 2013|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-a-litmus-test-for-both-pas-and-umno|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131104212904/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/sungai-limau-a-litmus-test-for-both-pas-and-umno|archive-date=4 November 2013|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref>

The by election was won by Mohd Azam Samad with a reduced majority of 1,084 votes.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Barisan Nasional fails to win Sungai Limau, PAS majority reduced|date=4 November 2013|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pas-retains-sungai-limau-with-a-reduced-majority|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150927075417/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pas-retains-sungai-limau-with-a-reduced-majority|archive-date=27 September 2015}}</ref>
