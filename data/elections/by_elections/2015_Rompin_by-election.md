A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] seat of '''[[Rompin (federal constituency)|Rompin]]''', [[Pahang]] in [[Malaysia]] on 5 May following the nomination day on 22 April 2015.<ref>{{cite news|work=[[The Star (Malaysia)]]|title=Rompin by-election: It's veteran versus youth |date=22 April 2015|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/News/Nation/2015/04/22/rompin-by-election-straight-fight-BN-and-PAS/}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after member of parliament [[Jamaluddin Jarjis]], from [[Barisan Nasional]] died in a helicopter crash in [[Semenyih]], [[Selangor]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2015/04/05/chopper-crash-bodies-of-rompin-mp-jamaluddin-jarjis-pms-aide-azlin-alias-identified/ |title=Chopper crash: Bodies of Rompin MP Jamaluddin Jarjis, PM's aide Azlin Alias identified - The Rakyat Post - The Rakyat Post |website=www.therakyatpost.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150405235638/http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2015/04/05/chopper-crash-bodies-of-rompin-mp-jamaluddin-jarjis-pms-aide-azlin-alias-identified/ |archive-date=2015-04-05}}</ref> Jamaluddin Jarjis won the seat with a majority of 15,114 votes in the [[2013 Malaysian general election|general elections in 2013]], beating [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party]] (PAS)'s Mohd Nuridah Salleh.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bernama.com/bernama/v8/ge/newsgeneral.php?id=1128302 |title=BERNAMA - Nomination Process For Rompin Parliamentary By-Election Opens |website=www.bernama.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304131020/http://www.bernama.com/bernama/v8/ge/newsgeneral.php?id=1128302 |archive-date=2016-03-04}}</ref>

The Rompin by-election was a straight contest between ruling party [[United Malays National Organisation]] (UMNO)'s [[Hasan Arifin]] and opposition PAS's Nazri Ahmad.<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/straight-fight-between-bn-and-pas-in-rompin| title = Straight fight between BN and PAS in Rompin {{!}} Malaysia {{!}} Malay Mail}}</ref>

BN held the seat, albeit with their majority slashed by almost half.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=BN retains Rompin, majority reduced by almost half|date=5 May 2015|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bn-set-to-retain-rompin|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150507171655/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bn-set-to-retain-rompin|archive-date=7 May 2015}}</ref>
