A '''by-election''' was held on 25 August 2009 for the [[Penang State Legislative Assembly]] seat of '''[[Permatang Pasir (state constituency)|Permatang Pasir]]'''.<ref name=:"1">{{Cite web| title= Permatang Pasir by-election set for Aug 25|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2009/08/11/permatang-pasir-byelection-set-for-aug-25|access-date=2024-05-24|website=The Star |language=en}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after the death of the incumbent assemblyman, Mohd Hamdan Abdul Rahman.<ref name=:"2">{{Cite web| title= ADUN PAS meninggal dunia|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/109632|access-date=2024-05-24|website=malaysiakini |language=ms}}</ref>

==Result==

Mohd Salleh Man got 9,618 votes, beating Rohaizat Othman who got 5,067 votes.<ref name=:"3">{{Cite web| title= Permatang Pasir result a big boost for PAS, PR|url=https://theedgemalaysia.com/article/permatang-pasir-result-big-boost-pas-pr|access-date=2024-05-24|website=The Edge Malaysia |language=en}}</ref>
