By-elections in Kinabatangan are scheduled to be held on 24 January 2026 for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] seat of '''[[Kinabatangan (federal constituency)|Kinabatangan]]'''. It was called following the death of [[Bung Moktar Radin]] on 5 December 2025.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Vanar |first=Mugunthan |date=5 December 2025 |title=Sabah BN chairman Bung passes away |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2025/12/05/sabah-bn-chairman-bung-passes-away |access-date=5 December 2025 |work=The Star}}</ref> Bung served as the Kinabatangan MP from 1999. The by-election is held concurrently with the [[2026 Lamag by-election]], the state seat Bung held.

The election is the fourth federal by-election since GE15 and part of the second set of by-elections where an incumbent death led to simultaneous by-elections in both parliamentary and state constituencies since [[2023 Pulai by-election|Pulai]] in 2023.{{#tag:ref|Not counting the federal by-election for [[Padang Serai (federal constituency)|Padang Serai]] and state by-election for [[Tioman (state constituency)|Tioman]], both of which were delayed to 7 December 2022 because of the death of the respective candidates after nomination day for GE15. Both by-elections and results are considered as part of the [[2022 Malaysian general election|2022 general election]] and [[2022 Pahang state election]].|group=note}}

==Background==
Kinbatangan is a Sabah Bumiputera-majority seat comprising 65.0% of the overall voters, followed by 27.6% Malay-Muslim Bumiputera, 5.40% of other ethnicities and 2.0% Chinese.

Bung Moktar died on 5 December 2025 at Kota Kinabalu Gleneagles Hospital,<ref name=":0" /> after being hospitalized for kidney failure and a lung infection.<ref>{{Cite news |date=5 December 2025 |title=Bung Moktar hospitalised with kidney failure and severe lung infection - Jafry |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2025/12/1330057/bung-moktar-hospitalised-kidney-failure-and-severe-lung-infection-jafry |access-date=5 December 2025 |work=New Straits Times}}</ref>

==Nomination==
On 19 December 2025, [[Barisan Nasional]] named [[Naim Moktar|Mohd Kurniawan Naim Moktar]], the son of Bung Mokhtar, as their candidate for the seat.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Bung Mokhtar's son named BN candidate for Kinabatangan by-election|url=https://www.bernama.com/en/news.php/politics/news.php?id=2504690|last=|first=|date=20 December 2025|newspaper=[[Bernama]]|access-date=20 December 2025|language=en}}</ref> [[Heritage Party (Malaysia)|Warisan]] nominated former [[Sukau (state constituency)|Sukau]] assemblyman Saddi Abdul Rahman as their candidate on 5 January 2026.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Warisan announces candidates for Kinabatangan, Lamag by-elections |date=2026-01-05 |url=https://theedgemalaysia.com/node/788057 |access-date=2026-01-06 |work=[[The Edge]] |language=en}}</ref> Three days later, lawyer Goldam Hamid announced his intention of running as independent.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Lawyer enters Kinabatangan by-election contest |last=Sidom |first=Poliana Ronnie |date=2026-01-08 |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2026/01/1353126/lawyer-enters-kinabatangan-election-contest |access-date=2026-01-20 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |language=en}}</ref>

[[Gabungan Rakyat Sabah]] announced it would not contest the seat out of respect for Bung.<ref>{{Cite news|title=GRS unlikely to contest Kinabatangan, Lamag by‑elections out of respect for Bung Moktar, says Hajiji|url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2025/12/16/grs-unlikely-to-contest-kinabatangan-lamag-byelections-out-of-respect-for-bung-moktar-says-hajiji/202160|last=Chan|first=Julia|date=16 December 2025|newspaper=[[The Malay Mail]]|access-date=17 December 2025|language=en}}</ref> [[Perikatan Nasional]] similarly also decided not to contest both the Kinabatangan and Lamag seats.<ref>{{Cite news|title=PN to sit out Kinabatangan, Lamag by-elections in Sabah|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/regional/2025/12/1334040/pn-sit-out-kinabatangan-lamag-elections-sabah|last=Mahsinah Abdullah|first=Sharifah|date=10 December 2025|newspaper=[[New Straits Times]]|access-date=20 December 2025|language=en}}</ref>
