The '''Galas by-election''' was an election for the [[Galas]] seat in the [[Kelantan]] state legislative assembly to replace the incumbent, who died in office. Polling was held on 4 November 2010. Nominations were held on 26 October 2010. The Galas state seat fell vacant following the death of assemblyman Che Hashim Sulaiman on 27 September due to cancer. Previously, PAS won the seat during the 2008 General Election by a 646-vote majority over Barisan Nasional's Saufi Deraman.

The electorate is made of 61.63% Malays, 20.08% Chinese, 16.37% Orang Asli, 1.6% Indians, 0.09% others.<ref>http://www.thesundaily.com/article.cfm?id=52596{{Dead link|date=April 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Galas: Polling day on Nov 4, nominations Oct 26</ref><ref>http://www.allvoices.com/contributed-news/6957348-election-at-galas-malaysia {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120313122912/http://www.allvoices.com/contributed-news/6957348-election-at-galas-malaysia |date=2012-03-13 }} Election at Galas, Malaysia</ref> Barisan Nasional's candidate won the by-election.

The by-election was held on the same day as the [[2010 Batu Sapi by-election]].
