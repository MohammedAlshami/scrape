}}A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] seat of '''P93 [[Sungai Besar]]''', a parliamentary seat located in the state of [[Selangor]], [[Malaysia]] on 18 June 2016 following the nomination day on 5 June 2016.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Malaysiakini|accessdate=27 May 2016|title=Sungai Besar, K Kangsar by-elections on June 18|date=13 May 2016|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/341368}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after death of member of parliament [[Noriah Kasnon]], who died in a helicopter crash on May 5, 2016 while campaigning in the [[2016 Sarawak state election|Sarawak state election]].<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Malaysiakini|accessdate=27 May 2016|title=Sungai Besar, Kuala Kangsar set for by-elections|date=10 May 2016|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/340934}}</ref> Noriah Kasnon was also the [[Ministry of Plantation Industries and Commodities (Malaysia)|Plantations Industries and Commodities]] Deputy Minister.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=New Straits Times|accessdate=27 May 2016|title=Confirmed: Noriah Kasnon among six on board missing chopper|date=6 May 2016| url=http://www.nst.com.my/news/2016/05/143850/confirmed-noriah-kasnon-among-six-board-missing-chopper}}</ref> In the [[2013 Malaysian general election|2013 general election]], Noriah Kasnon won the seat with a majority of 399 votes beating Mohamed Salleh M Hussin of [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party]] (PAS).<ref>{{cite news| publisher=Malaysiakini|accessdate=3 June 2016|title=Hisham: BN doesn't take Sungai Besar, Kuala Kangsar by-elections lightly|date=16 May 2016| url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/341727}}</ref> This by-election was held concurrently with the [[2016 Kuala Kangsar by-election|Kuala Kangsar by-election]] for the same reason.

The Barisan Nasional candidate was [[Budiman Mohd Zohdi]] from [[United Malays National Organisation]] (UMNO) who is also the [[Sungai Panjang (state constituency)|Sungai Panjang]] state assemblyman.<ref>{{cite news|work=The Star Online|accessdate=28 May 2016|title=BN's Sungai Besar candidate Budiman prefers to be underdog|date=27 May 2016| url=http://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2016/05/27/the-war-has-begun-says-bn-sungai-besar-candidate-budiman/}}</ref> The [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party|PAS]] candidate is Abdul Rani Osman who is also the state assemblyman for [[Meru (state constituency)|Meru]].<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Astro Awani|accessdate=28 May 2016|title=Dr Abdul Rani is Pas candidate for Sungai Besar|work=Bernama|date=28 May 2016| url=http://english.astroawani.com/politics-news/dr-abdul-rani-pas-candidate-sungai-besar-106578}}</ref> The opposition coalition [[Pakatan Harapan]] has chosen Azhar Abdul Shukor from the [[National Trust Party (Malaysia)|National Trust Party]] (AMANAH) as its candidate.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Malay Mail Online|accessdate=2 June 2016|title=Local boy Azhar Shukur is Amanah's pick for Sungai Besar|date=1 June 2016| url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/local-boy-azhar-shukur-is-amanahs-pick-for-sungai-besar}}</ref>

Some of the issues that will be brought up during the by-election campaign are the [[Goods and Services Tax (Malaysia)|Goods and Services tax]], the rising cost of living and electricity tariffs.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thesundaily.my/news/1818903|title = GST and cost of electricity main causes of concern for Sungai Besar residents}}</ref>

== Results ==
Budiman Mohd Zohdi retained the seat for Barisan Nasional with a majority 9,191 votes beating both candidates from Amanah and PAS.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Free Malaysia Today|accessdate=18 June 2016|title=BN retains Sungai Besar with huge majority|date=18 June 2016|url=http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2016/06/18/bn-retains-sungai-besar-with-increased-majority/|archive-date=17 February 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200217194028/https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2016/06/18/bn-retains-sungai-besar-with-increased-majority/|url-status=dead}}</ref>
