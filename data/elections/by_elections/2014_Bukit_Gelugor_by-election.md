A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] parliamentary seat of '''[[Bukit Gelugor (federal constituency)|Bukit Gelugor]]''' on 25 May 2014 following the nomination day on 12 May 2014.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Bukit Gelugor by-election set for May 25, nominations May 12, says polls body|date=24 April 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bukit-gelugor-by-election-set-for-may-25-nominations-may-12-says-polls-body|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140424133624/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bukit-gelugor-by-election-set-for-may-25-nominations-may-12-says-polls-body#|archive-date=2014-04-24|url-status=dead}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after the passing of the incumbent MP, [[Karpal Singh]] in a motor accident near [[Kampar, Perak|Kampar]], [[Perak]] on 17 April 2014.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Karpal Singh dies in road accident, son injured|date=17 April 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/karpal-singh-dies-in-road-accidentt|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140419032437/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/karpal-singh-dies-in-road-accidentt#|archive-date=2014-04-19|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[New Straits Times]] |title=Karpal Singh's Death: Bukit Gelugor MP killed in crash, son injured |date=17 April 2014 |url=http://www.nst.com.my/latest/font-color-red-karpal-singh-s-death-font-bukit-gelugor-mp-killed-in-crash-son-injured-1.570474 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140417165019/http://www.nst.com.my/latest/font-color-red-karpal-singh-s-death-font-bukit-gelugor-mp-killed-in-crash-son-injured-1.570474 |archive-date=2014-04-17 }}</ref> 

==Background==
Karpal hailed from the [[Democratic Action Party]], a component party of [[Pakatan Rakyat]].<ref>{{cite news|work=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|title=Karpal Singh: One and only Tiger|date=17 April 2014|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/News/Nation/2014/04/17/karpal-singh-one-and-only-tiger/}}</ref> A political and legal veteran, he served in the Parliament since 1978 (except for a brief period) and as the chairman of the DAP from 2004 until 2014. He won by a majority of 41,778 votes against Barisan Nasional (Malaysian Chinese Association) candidate Teh Beng Yeam in the [[2013 Malaysian general election|2013 general election]].<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malay Mail]]|title=Karpal's Bukit Gelugor seat will stay DAP's, analysts say|date=24 April 2014|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/karpals-bukit-gelugor-seat-will-stay-daps-analysts-say}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://undi.info/penang/p/P51 |title=Undi.info |access-date=2014-04-17 |archive-date=2014-04-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140419012316/http://undi.info/penang/p/P51 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Nominations and campaign==
The DAP fielded [[Ramkarpal Singh]], Karpal's son and a lawyer.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Ramkarpal to defend dad's Bukit Gelugor seat|date=10 May 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/ramkarpal-to-defend-dads-seat|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140513174703/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/ramkarpal-to-defend-dads-seat#|archive-date=2014-05-13|url-status=dead}}</ref> Hours after Ramkarpal's candidacy was announced, MCA decided to defer from contesting the by-election thus leaving BN with no candidate in the by-election.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[Malaysiakini]]|title=MCA to stay clear of Bukit Gelugor rumble|date=10 May 2014|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/262413}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Bukit Gelugor by-election will only spell humiliating defeat for MCA, say analysts|date=12 May 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bukit-gelugor-by-election-would-only-spell-humiliating-defeat-for-mca-say-a|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140515025627/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/bukit-gelugor-by-election-would-only-spell-humiliating-defeat-for-mca-say-a#|archive-date=2014-05-15|url-status=dead}}</ref> BN chief and Prime Minister Najib Razak indicated that BN would instead throw support on other candidates. [[Parti Cinta Malaysia]] vice-president and former BN MP for Batu Kawan Huan Cheng Guan contested in the by-election along with two independents Mohamed Nabi Bux Mohamed Abdul Sathar and Abu Backer Sidek Mohammad Zan.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=In Barisan's absence, DAP can expect easy win in Bukit Gelugor|date=12 May 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/with-barisans-absence-dap-can-expect-easy-win-in-bukit-gelugor|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140513171601/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/with-barisans-absence-dap-can-expect-easy-win-in-bukit-gelugor#|archive-date=2014-05-13|url-status=dead}}</ref> Abu Backer withdrew a day before the by-election, although his decision was not recognized by the Electoral Commission.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[Free Malaysia Today]]|title=Never-ending drama from Abu Backer|date=24 May 2014|url=http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2014/05/24/never-ending-drama-from-abu-backer}}</ref> The number of eligible voters in this constituency was 82,431.

== Results ==
Ramkarpal Singh won the by-election with a majority of 37,659 votes compared to his nearest challenger. The other three candidates lost their deposit after failing to receive at least one-eighth of the votes. The turnout in the by-election is 30% lower compared to the turnout in the [[2013 Malaysian general election|2013 general election]].<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=It's official… Ramkarpal wins Bukit Gelugor, challengers lose their deposit|date=25 May 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/election-commission-declares-ramkarpal-winner-in-bukit-gelugor|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140527203001/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/election-commission-declares-ramkarpal-winner-in-bukit-gelugor#|archive-date=2014-05-27|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[Free Malaysia Today]]|title=Ramkarpal falls short of dad's majority|date=25 May 2014|url=http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2014/05/25/ramkarpal-falls-short-of-dads-majority/|access-date=25 May 2014|archive-date=25 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140525200621/http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2014/05/25/ramkarpal-falls-short-of-dads-majority/|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[Malaysiakini]]|script-title=zh:行动党3万7千多数票守土蓝卡巴：国会，我来了！|date=25 May 2014|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/263833|language=zh}}</ref>
