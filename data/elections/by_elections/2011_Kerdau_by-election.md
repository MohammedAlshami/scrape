The '''2011 [[Kerdau]] by-election''' is a state by-election that was scheduled held on 6 March 2011 in the state of [[Pahang]], [[Malaysia]]. The nomination of candidates was done on 26 February 2011. The Kerdau seat fell vacant following the death of its state assemblyman Datuk Zaharuddin Abu Kassim of [[United Malays National Organisation]], part of the [[Barisan Nasional]] coalition from a heart attack at his home.<ref>http://www.mysinchew.com/node/53448 Kerdau polling on March 6</ref><ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20110213184850/http://thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=%2F2011%2F2%2F13%2Fnation%2F8057781&sec=nation] Kerdau rep dies, 16th by-election on the cards</ref> Previously Zaharuddin Abu Kassim won the Kerdau seat with a 1,615 vote majority, beating [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party|PAS]]' Hassanuddin Salim at the [[2008 Malaysian general election]]s.<ref>http://www.nst.com.my/nst/articles/9kerd-3/Article/ Kerdau rep dies after fall at home</ref> The state assembly seat has 8,721 voters registered compromising of 88.36% [[Malaysian Malays|Malays]], 3.6% [[Malaysian Chinese|Chinese]], 3.4% [[Malaysian Indian|Indians]] and 2.98% other races.<ref>http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/2011/02/17/laluan-sukar-buat-pr-tawan-kerdau/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110711032040/http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/2011/02/17/laluan-sukar-buat-pr-tawan-kerdau/ |date=2011-07-11 }} Laluan sukar buat Pakatan tawan Kerdau</ref> For the by-election PAS picked as its candidate, Hassanuddin Salim while Barisan Nasional picked Syed Ibrahim Syed Ahmad.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20110628191549/http://thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=%2F2011%2F2%2F27%2Fmerlimau_kerdau%2F8148704&sec=merlimau_kerdau] Straight fights in Merlimau and Kerdau by-elections</ref>

== Results ==
Syed Ibrahim Syed Ahmad, the Barisan candidate won by polling 5,060 votes against the 2,336 garnered by Hassanuddin Salim of PAS.<ref>http://www.mysinchew.com/node/54287 BN winning streak in by-elections continues</ref>
