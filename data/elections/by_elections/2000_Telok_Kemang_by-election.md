The '''2000 Telok Kemang by-election''' was a by-election that was held on 10 June 2000 for the [[Dewan Rakyat]] seat of [[Telok Kemang (federal constituency)|Telok Kemang]], [[Negeri Sembilan]]. It was called following the death of its Member of Parliament, S. A. Anpalagan on 28th April 2000. Anpalagan won the seat on [[1999 Malaysian general election]] with a 9,942 majority.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Barisan only looking at the margin of victory in Telok Kemang |last=Salleh |first=Kamarulzaman |date=2000-06-09 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000609&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=2 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=Mohd Isa satisifed with the victory |date=2000-06-11 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000611&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=2 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=Chembong, Linggi undergoing transformation |date=2000-06-09 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000609&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=4 |language=en}}</ref>

[[S. Sothinathan]] of [[Barisan Nasional]] won the seat against [[Parti Keadilan Nasional|KeADILan]] candidate Ruslan Kasim with a majority of 5,972 votes.The seat have 66,646 registered voters.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Barisan's Sothinathan wins seat |date=2000-06-11 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000611&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=1 |language=en}}</ref>

==Nomination==
Nomination day is on 30th May 2000. [[Barisan Nasional]] nominated [[Malaysian Indian Congress|MIC]] president political secretary, [[S. Sothinathan]]. KeADILan nominated its information chief and [[Negeri Sembilan]] chairman, Ruslan Kasim.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Sothinathan thanks voters for having confidence in him |date=2000-06-11 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000611&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=2 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=Telok Kemang defeat a bitter pill for opposition to swallow |last=Akmar| first=Shamsul |date=2000-06-11 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=x8G803Bi31IC&dat=20000611&printsec=frontpage&hl=en |access-date=2024-12-11 |work=[[New Straits Times]] |pages=2 |language=en}}</ref>
