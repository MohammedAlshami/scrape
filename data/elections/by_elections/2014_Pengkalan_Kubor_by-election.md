A '''by-election''' was held for the [[Kelantan State Legislative Assembly|Kelantan State Assembly]] seat of '''[[Pengkalan Kubur|Pengkalan Kubor]]''' on 25 September 2014 following the nomination day on 13 September 2014.<ref name=TMI>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Pengkalan Kubor by-election on September 25|date=29 August 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pengkalan-kubor-by-election-on-september-25|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140902003618/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pengkalan-kubor-by-election-on-september-25|archive-date=2 September 2014}}</ref> The seat fell vacant after the death of the incumbent three-term assemblyman, [[Noor Zahidi Omar]] from liver cancer in [[Guangzhou]], [[China]] on 20 August 2014.<ref>{{cite news|publisher=[[The Malaysian Insider]]|title=Pengkalan Kubor rep dies in China|date=20 August 2014|url=http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pengkalan-kubor-rep-dies-in-china|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140823041607/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/pengkalan-kubor-rep-dies-in-china|archive-date=23 August 2014}}</ref> Zahidi was an assemblyman from the [[United Malays National Organisation]], a component party of the opposition [[Barisan Nasional]] coalition. In the [[2013 Malaysian general election|2013 general election]], he defeated [[Pakatan Rakyat]] ([[People's Justice Party (Malaysia)|PKR]]) candidate Saharun Ibrahim and an independent candidate by 1,736 votes.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.myundi.com.my/pru13/kelantan.aspx |title=Keputusan PRU13 Kawasan Kelantan |publisher=my undi |language=ms |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140829082041/http://www.myundi.com.my/pru13/kelantan.aspx |archive-date=2014-08-29 }}</ref>

Mat Razi Mat Ail from [[United Malays National Organisation|UMNO]] represented Barisan Nasional.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2014/09/13/three-cornered-fight-for-pengkalan-kubor/ |title = Three-cornered fight for Pengkalan Kubor {{!}} Free Malaysia Today  |access-date=2014-09-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140913195005/http://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2014/09/13/three-cornered-fight-for-pengkalan-kubor/ |archive-date=2014-09-13 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Pakatan Rakyat was represented by Wan Rosdi Wan Ibrahim from [[Pan-Malaysian Islamic Party|PAS]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2014/09/11/wan-rosdi-pas-man-pengkalan-kubor/ |title=Wan Rosdi is PAS' man in Pengkalan Kubor - the Rakyat Post - the Rakyat Post |access-date=2014-09-13 |archive-date=2014-09-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140913201752/http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2014/09/11/wan-rosdi-pas-man-pengkalan-kubor/ |url-status=dead }}</ref> Independent candidate Izat Bukhary Ismail Bukhary will also be contesting the seat.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/three-cornered-fight-for-pengkalan-kubor-state-seat|title=Three-cornered fight for Pengkalan Kubor state seat {{pipe}} Malay Mail|date=27 October 2022 }}</ref> A total of 24,039 voters can vote in the by-election.<ref name=TMI />
