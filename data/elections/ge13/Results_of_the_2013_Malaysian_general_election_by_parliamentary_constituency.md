These are the '''election results of the [[2013 Malaysian general election]] by parliamentary constituency'''.<ref>Election Results of the 13th Malaysian General Elections {{cite web |url=http://keputusan.spr.gov.my/ |title=Dashboard PRU13 |access-date=2013-04-23 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130423092454/http://keputusan.spr.gov.my/ |archive-date=2013-04-23 }}</ref><ref>Star Online Election Results http://elections.thebgcolorstar.com.my/results/results.aspx{{Dead link|date=July 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>Sabah Parliament&mdash;Official Results at a Glance http://borneoinsider.com/sabah-parliament-official-results-at-a-glance/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130508143233/http://borneoinsider.com/sabah-parliament-official-results-at-a-glance/ |date=2013-05-08 }}</ref><ref>New Straits Times Election Results http://www.nst.com.my/ge13</ref> These [[Members of the Dewan Rakyat, 13th Malaysian Parliament|members of parliament]] (MPs) will be representing their constituency from the first sitting of 13th Malaysian Parliament to its dissolution.

The parliamentary election deposit was set at [[Malaysian ringgit|RM]] 10,000 per candidate. Similar to previous elections, the election deposit will be forfeited if the particular candidate had failed to secure at least 12.5% or one-eighth of the votes.
