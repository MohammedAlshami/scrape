These are the '''election results of the [[2020 Sabah state election]]'''. [[State legislative assemblies of Malaysia|State assembly]] elections were held in [[Sabah]], [[Malaysia]] on 26 September 2020 as part of the snap elections. Results are expected to come on the same day, after 5 pm. [[List of Malaysian State Assembly Representatives (2018–)|Elected members of the legislative assembly]] (MLAs) will be representing their constituency from the first sitting of respective state legislative assembly to its dissolution.

The state legislature election deposit was set at [[Malaysian ringgit|RM]]5,000 per candidate. Similar to previous elections, the election deposit will be forfeited if the particular candidate had failed to secure at least 12.5% or one-eighth of the votes.
