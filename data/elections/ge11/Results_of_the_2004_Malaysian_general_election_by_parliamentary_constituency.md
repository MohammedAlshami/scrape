These are the '''election results of the [[2004 Malaysian general election]] by parliamentary constituency'''. These [[Members of the Dewan Rakyat, 11th Malaysian Parliament|members of parliament]] (MPs) representing their constituency from the first sitting of 11th Malaysian Parliament to its dissolution.

The parliamentary election deposit was set at [[Malaysian ringgit|RM]] 10,000 per candidate. Similar to previous elections, the election deposit will be forfeited if the particular candidate had failed to secure at least 12.5% or one-eighth of the votes.
