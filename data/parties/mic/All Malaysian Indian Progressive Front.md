The '''All Malaysia Indian Progressive Front''' ({{langx|ms|Barisan Kemajuan India Se-Malaysia}}), [[abbreviation|abbrev]]: '''AMIPF''', or better known just as the ''Indian Progressive Front'' ''(IPF)'', is a [[Malaysia]]n political party. It is a splinter party of the [[Malaysian Indian Congress]] (MIC) formed by its dissident leader [[M. G. Pandithan]] in 1990. The party was a component of the defunct opposition coalition, [[Gagasan Rakyat]] (GR) from 1990 to 1996, but it currently supports the [[Barisan Nasional]] (BN) coalition although it is not a component member.<ref>{{cite news|title=IPF Still Hopeful Of Joining BN|date=30 July 2010|work=[[Bernama]]}}</ref> IPF had failed in its application to join BN after an objection from MIC.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2017/10/08/ipf-seeks-to-join-bn-moots-two-day-break-for-deepavali/1482607|title=IPF seeks to join BN, moots two-day break for Deepavali|author= Ida Lim|publisher=[[Malay Mail]]|date=8 October 2017|access-date=16 July 2018}}</ref> Instead it is being considered just as a 'Friends of BN' party.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.indianmalaysian.com/sound/modules.php?name=News&file=print&sid=977 |title=IPF To Support BN Candidates In General Election - Pandithan, 31 December 2007, Bernama |access-date=27 April 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190427150929/http://www.indianmalaysian.com/sound/modules.php?name=News |archive-date=27 April 2019 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2018/10/27/zahid-three-indian-parties-may-join-barisan |title=Zahid: Three small Indian parties - Kimma, IPF and Makkal Sakti - may join Barisan|work=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]] |date=27 October 2018|accessdate=7 June 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://facebook.com/mediaumnomalaysia/photos/a.4225231564169226/4225234747502241/?type=3|title=Perjumpaan Bersama Parti Friends of BN|work=UMNO ONLINE |language=ms|via=[[Facebook]]|date=13 March 2020|accessdate=7 June 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://umno-online.my/2020/03/13/bersama-parti-friends-of-bn/|title=Bersama Parti Friends of BN|work=UMNO ONLINE |language=ms|date=13 March 2020 |accessdate=7 June 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.sinarharian.com.my/article/116662/BERITA/Politik/Selesai-dahulu-masalah-permohonan-parti-politik-lain-Penganalisis|title=Selesai dahulu masalah permohonan parti politik lain: Penganalisis|work=[[Sinar Harian]]|language=ms |date=29 December 2020 |accessdate=7 June 2021}}</ref> Prime Minister [[Mahathir Mohamad]] said the party has played a positive role in enhancing the image of Barisan Nasional, especially among the Indian community.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2001-02-10 |title=IPF played vital role, says PM |url=https://www.klik.com.my/item/story/1550243/ipf-played-vital-role-says-pm |website=New Straits Times}}</ref>
