A '''leadership election''' will be held by the [[Malaysian United Indigenous Party]] (BERSATU) on 2 November 2024.<ref name="Hamzah-Azmin proxy war in Bersatu elections, says insider
">{{cite web|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2024/10/07/hamzah-azmin-proxy-war-in-bersatu-elections-says-insider/|title=Hamzah-Azmin proxy war in Bersatu elections, says insider|work=Free Malaysia Today|access-date=13 October 2024}}</ref><ref name="Pemilihan Bersatu mungkin tahun depan - Muhyiddin">{{cite web|url=https://www.astroawani.com/berita-politik/pemilihan-bersatu-mungkin-tahun-depan-muhyiddin-446603|title=Pemilihan Bersatu mungkin tahun depan - Muhyiddin|work=Astro Awani|access-date=25 November 2023}}</ref> This is the second BERSATU leadership election after the [[2020 Malaysian United Indigenous Party leadership election|2020 leadership election]].

== Events ==
Incumbent President of BERSATU [[Muhyiddin Yassin]] confirmed on 25 November 2023 that he would defend his presidency after announcing he would not do so a day earlier.<ref name="Perhimpunan Bersatu: Muhyiddin tarik balik keputusan bersara">{{cite web|url=https://www.utusan.com.my/nasional/2023/11/perhimpunan-bersatu-muhyiddin-tarik-baik-keputusan-bersara/|title=Perhimpunan Bersatu: Muhyiddin tarik balik keputusan bersara|work=Utusan Malaysia|date=25 November 2023 |access-date=25 November 2023}}</ref> On 6 July 2024, it was confirmed that incumbent Deputy President [[Ahmad Faizal Azumu]] and Secretary-General [[Hamzah Zainudin]] would compete for the deputy presidency. For the three vice presidencies, incumbent [[Ronald Kiandee]] and [[Radzi Jidin]] would defend their positions while 10 others including incumbent Information Chief [[Razali Idris]], Spokesperson [[Wan Saiful Wan Jan]], State Chairman of [[Pahang]] [[Saifuddin Abdullah]] and Election Bureau Chairman [[Mohd Rafiq Mohd Abdullah]] have expressed their interest in contesting for the position.<ref name="Peja-Hamzah rebut timbalan presiden Bersatu, Azmin tekan ‘brek’?">{{cite web|url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2024/06/28/peja-hamzah-rebut-timbalan-presiden-bersatu-azmin-tekan-brek/|title=Peja-Hamzah rebut timbalan presiden Bersatu, Azmin tekan 'brek'?|work=Free Malaysia Today|date=27 June 2024 |access-date=6 July 2024}}</ref>

However, on 13 July 2024, Muhyiddin proposed a new leadership lineup that has been endorsed and agreed by all members of the Supreme Council and the [[annual general meeting]] (AGM) accepting Hamzah Zainudin's promotion to deputy president and Ahmad Faizal Azumu's demotion to vice president. While the other two incumbent vice presidents, [[Ronald Kiandee]] and [[Radzi Jidin|Mohd Radzi Md Jidin]], retained their positions. [[Azmin Ali]] will appointed as secretary-general of the party.<ref name="Hamzah baka">{{Cite web |last=AZIS |first=DIANA |date=2024-07-13 |title=[VIDEO] Hamzah bakal 'naik pangkat', Peja lepas jawatan Timbalan Presiden Bersatu |url=https://www.sinarharian.com.my/article/674882/berita/politik/video-hamzah-bakal-naik-pangkat-peja-lepas-jawatan-timbalan-presiden-bersatu |access-date=2024-07-13 |website=Sinar Harian |language=ms}}</ref><ref name="Pemilihan Bersatu: Peja undur diri, Hamzah bertanding Timbalan Presiden">{{Cite web |date=2024-07-13 |title=Pemilihan Bersatu: Peja undur diri, Hamzah bertanding Timbalan Presiden |url=https://www.astroawani.com/berita-politik/pemilihan-bersatu-peja-undur-diri-hamzah-bertanding-timbalan-presiden-478907 |access-date=2024-07-13 |website=Astro Awani |language=ms}}</ref><ref name="Bersatu polls will likely see no contest for top posts, says Muhyiddin">{{Cite web |date=2024-07-13 |title=Bersatu polls will likely see no contest for top posts, says Muhyiddin |url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/videos/5lRaOCypw0Y/bersatu-polls-will-likely-see-no-contest-for-top-posts-says-muhyiddin/?playlistId=PLKe9JQ8opkEAErOOqs4tB87iWhuh_-osl |access-date=2024-07-13 |website=Free Malaysia Today |language=en}}</ref>
