The '''Social Democratic Harmony Party''' ([[Malay language|Malay]]: ''Parti Kesejahteraan Demokratik Masyarakat''; [[abbreviation|abbrev]]: '''KDM''') is a regional political party based in [[Sabah]], [[Malaysia]] founded by [[Peter Anthony]] and [[Juil Nuatim]]. It aims to represent the interests of the [[Kadazan-Dusun]] and [[Murut people|Murut]] people. The KDM is currently aligned to the government as a partner, providing confidence and supply to the [[Gabungan Rakyat Sabah]] (GRS) state ruling coalition led by Chief Minister [[Hajiji Noor]]. The party's acronym, KDM, is also used to refer to the native Kadazan-Dusun and Murut communities.

== History ==
KDM was founded as a political party on 28 January 2022.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/01/16/new-political-party-to-be-launched-on-jan-28-says-ex-warisan-vp-peter-anthony|title=New political party to be launched on Jan 28, says ex-Warisan VP Peter Anthony|website=The Star}}</ref> Its founding President [[Peter Anthony]], considered a political strongman in [[Tenom]], was a Member of the [[Sabah State Legislative Assembly]] (MLA) for [[Melalap (state constituency)|Melalap]], [[Cabinet of Sabah|State Minister of Infrastructure Development of Sabah]], as well as a Vice-President of the [[Heritage Party (Malaysia)|Heritage Party]] (WARISAN).

Peter left WARISAN exactly a month before the founding of KDM on 28 December 2021 along with [[Limbahau (state constituency)|Limbahau]] MLA, Juil Nuatim.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2021/12/28/peter-expected-to-quit |title=Peter expected to quit |website=The Star}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2021/12/28/peter-anthony-quits-warisan |title=Peter Anthony quits Warisan |website=The Star}}</ref> He claimed WARISAN "had diverted from its original path of serving Sabah" by expanding to [[Peninsular Malaysia]], and denied that his exit was linked to an ongoing court case against him over false documents.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2021/12/29/shafie-peter-anthony-left-warisan-because-of-court-case-party039s-expansion-not-a-factor|title=Shafie: Peter Anthony left Warisan because of court case, party's expansion not a factor|website=The Star}}</ref> Following Juil and Peter's exit, they were approached by the [[Homeland Solidarity Party]] (STAR), a major Kadazan-Dusun Murut party, but decided to persist with founding a new party that would be aligned with the ruling [[Gabungan Rakyat Sabah]] (GRS) coalition.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2021/12/29/join-sabah-star-for-native-unity-jeffrey-kitingan-tells-peter-and-juil|title=Join Sabah STAR for native unity, Jeffrey Kitingan tells Peter and Juil|website=The Star}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/01/01/former-warisan-veep-to-go-ahead-with-forming-new-party|title=Former Warisan veep to go ahead with forming new party|website=The Star}}</ref>

It was officially registered with the Registrar of Societies (RoS) on 18 February 2022, with Peter as president.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/02/21/peter-gets-parti-kdm-registered-set-to-work-with-grs|title=Peter gets Parti KDM registered, set to work with GRS|website=The Star}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/02/22/ronnie-loh-revealed-as-one-of-five-parti-kdm-vice-presidents|title=Ronnie Loh revealed as one of five Parti KDM vice-presidents|website=The Star}}</ref> 

The party's official launch was officiated by GRS leader and Sabah Chief Minister [[Hajiji Noor]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/03/13/gabungan-rakyat-sabah-now-a-single-entity-says-hajiji|title = Gabungan Rakyat Sabah now a single entity, says Hajiji}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/03/13/up-to-hajiji-to-decide-if-kdm-can-be-part-of-grs-says-peter-anthony|title = Up to Hajiji to decide if KDM can be part of GRS, says Peter Anthony}}</ref> However, the party is not a member of the coalition, and its application has faced pushback from the [[United Sabah Party]] (PBS), another major Kadazan-Dusun Murut party.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2022/03/14/pbs-confirms-it-did-not-send-any-reps-to-parti-kdm-launch|title = PBS confirms it did not send any reps to Parti KDM launch}}</ref>

[[Priscella Peter]], the daughter of Peter Anthony, became president upon his conviction in court.<ref>{{Cite web |date=8 March 2025 |title=Peter Anthony’s daughter Priscella, 27, steps up as Sabah’s KDM party president after his conviction |url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2025/03/08/peter-anthonys-daughter-priscella-27-steps-up-as-sabahs-kdm-party-president-after-his-conviction/168929 |access-date=5 November 2025 |website=[[Malay Mail]] |language=en}}</ref>
