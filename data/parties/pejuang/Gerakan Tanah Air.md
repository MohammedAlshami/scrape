'''Gerakan Tanah Air''' (GTA, ''Homeland Movement'' or ''Homeland Party'') is an [[Political coalition|alliance]] of [[Malay people|Malay]] [[List of political parties in Malaysia|political parties]], founded in August 2022 by former [[Prime Minister of Malaysia]] [[Mahathir Mohamad]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Dr M unveils Malay-only Gerakan Tanah Air coalition |url=https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2022/08/04/dr-m-unveils-malay-only-gerakan-tanah-air-coalition/ |website=Free Malaysia Today |access-date=5 August 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Dr M announces new coalition of four parties, Gerakan Tanah Air |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/politics/2022/08/819394/dr-m-announces-new-coalition-four-parties-gerakan-tanah-air |website=New Straits Times |access-date=5 August 2022}}</ref>

Mahathir had announced during the coalition's founding that GTA will contest in the [[next Malaysian general election]] against the incumbent ruling coalition [[Barisan Nasional]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Gerakan Tanah Air gabungan parti politik Melayu menentang UMNO dalam PRU15 - Tun Mahathir |url=https://www.astroawani.com/berita-politik/gerakan-tanah-air-gabungan-parti-politik-melayu-menentang-umno-dalam-pru15-tun-mahathir-374500 |website=Astro Awani |access-date=5 August 2022}}</ref> On 6 September 2022, the coalition submitted registration documents to the [[Societies Act 1966|Registrar of Societies]] (RoS) to apply for registration.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.malaysianow.com/my/news/2022/09/06/gerakan-tanah-air-serah-dokumen-pendaftaran-kepada-ros|title=Gerakan Tanah Air serah dokumen pendaftaran kepada RoS|last=Bernama|date=September 6, 2022|website=MalaysiaNow|access-date=6 September 2022}}</ref> The coalition failed to win any parliament seat in the [[2022 Malaysian general election|2022 General Election.]]<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-23 |title=Dr Mahathir saddened by Pejuang rout in GE15, hopes winning party gets to form new govt |url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2022/11/23/dr-mahathir-saddened-by-pejuang-rout-in-ge15-hopes-winning-party-gets-to-form-new-govt/41469 |access-date=2022-11-27 |website=Malay Mail |language=en}}</ref>

==History==
On 4 August 2022, PEJUANG Chairman Mahathir Mohamad announced launch of Gerakan Tanah Air (GTA) coalition which aims to keep the country safe and stable by reducing the current gap between races. He said, it does not mean that the movement is racist but rather it is to focus against a Malay party that has gone astray at the moment. The basis of the establishment of the Homeland Movement was not to seize the property of the non-Malay community or prevent them from trying to get wealth, but rather it sought to correct the economy of the Malay community. GTA comprises four parties – PEJUANG, Parti Bumiputera Perkasa Malaysia (Putra), Barisan Jemaah Islamiah Se-Malaysia (Berjasa) and the Parti Perikatan India Muslim Nasional (Iman).<ref>{{cite web |title=Dr M umum pelancaran Gerakan Tanah Air |url=https://www.bharian.com.my/berita/nasional/2022/08/984379/dr-m-umum-pelancaran-gerakan-tanah-air |website=[[Berita Harian]] |date=2022-08-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221007185818/https://www.bharian.com.my/berita/nasional/2022/08/984379/dr-m-umum-pelancaran-gerakan-tanah-air |archive-date=7 October 2022 |url-status=live |last1=Ruzki |first1=Rafidah |access-date=4 October 2022 }}</ref>

On 23 September 2022, Gerakan Tanah Air's (GTA) application to register as an official political party coalition has received preliminary approval from the Registrar of Society (RoS).<ref>{{cite web |title=Gerakan Tanah Air gets preliminary RoS approval |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/politics/2022/09/833769/gerakan-tanah-air-gets-preliminary-ros-approval |website=[[New Straits Times]] |date=2022-09-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230115194353/https://www.nst.com.my/news/politics/2022/09/833769/gerakan-tanah-air-gets-preliminary-ros-approval |archive-date=15 January 2023 |url-status=live |last1=Zulkifli |first1=Adie |access-date=15 January 2023 }}</ref> 

On 2 November 2022, Gerakan Tanah Air's (GTA) announced the 121 Parliamentary candidates who will compete to represent the movement in the 15th General Election (GE-15).<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.hmetro.com.my/mutakhir/2022/11/898950/pru-15-gta-bertanding-121-parlimen-metrotv | title=PRU-15: GTA bertanding 121 parlimen &#91;METROTV&#93; | date=2 November 2022 }}</ref> However, GTA failed to win all 121 Parliamentary chair.

On 14 January 2023, PEJUANG left GTA. PEJUANG President Mukhriz Mahathir said PEJUANG need focus on strengthening the party before adding value to any coalition. The decision was made after taking into account the views of the representatives in the second PEJUANG General Assembly, following the party's heavy defeat in the past 15th General Election (GE15). However, Mukhriz said PEJUANG took the stance of remaining open to hold any negotiations with any existing political coalition in the run-up to the State Election (PRN) which is expected to take place this year.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/651628 | title=Pejuang keluar GTA - Mukhriz | date=14 January 2023 }}</ref>

On 12 May 2023, Mahathir left GTA. Mahathir reached that decision after realising that GTA could not function properly and failed to attract voters' support, especially during the 15th general election (GE15) in November 2022.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2023/05/12/dr-mahathir-confirms-he-has-left-gta-does-not-hold-any-position-in-the-coalition/68832 | title=Dr Mahathir confirms he has left GTA, does not hold any position in the coalition | date=12 May 2023 }}</ref>
