A '''leadership election''' was held by the [[United Malays National Organisation]] (UMNO) party on 24 April 1987.<ref name="malaysia-today">{{cite web|url=http://www.malaysia-today.net/the-day-of-the-dictator-oct-27-1987/|title=The day of the dictator – Oct 27, 1987 &#124; Malaysia Today|publisher=malaysia-today.net|accessdate=2015-09-25|archive-date=2017-10-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171029174259/http://www.malaysia-today.net/the-day-of-the-dictator-oct-27-1987/|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="malaysiawaves">{{cite web|url=http://www.malaysiawaves.net/2010/05/1987-allah-swt-turunkan-bala-pada.html|title=Malaysia Waves: 1987: Mahathir Adalah Bala Allah Ke Atas Rakyat Malaysia|publisher=malaysiawaves.net|accessdate=2015-09-25|archive-date=2015-09-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150926020905/http://www.malaysiawaves.net/2010/05/1987-allah-swt-turunkan-bala-pada.html|url-status=dead}}</ref> It was won by incumbent Prime Minister and President of UMNO, [[Mahathir Mohamad]].
