A '''leadership election''' was held by the [[United Malays National Organisation]] (UMNO) party on 18 March 2023.

==Background==
{{Further|2020–2022 Malaysian political crisis|2022 Malaysian general election}}
In the aftermath of the [[2022 Malaysian general election]], [[Khairy Jamaluddin]], [[Noh Omar]], [[Shahril Sufian Hamdan]] and several UMNO leaders called for party leader [[Ahmad Zahid Hamidi]] to resign.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Mohamed Basyir |date=November 20, 2022 |title=Khairy: Zahid must resign now as Umno's reform cannot be delayed anymore |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/politics/2022/11/853095/khairy-zahid-must-resign-now-umnos-reform-cannot-be-delayed-anymore}}</ref> 

==Controversies==
On 14 January 2023, the UMNO general assembly has given consent to a motion for the posts of president and deputy president not to be contested at the coming party polls. Therefore, [[President of UMNO]], [[Ahmad Zahid Hamidi]] and Deputy President of UMNO, [[Mohamad Hasan (politician)|Mohamad Hasan]] will remain at their position respectively for another term until 2026.<ref name="Umno assembly gives the nod to no-contest motion, Zahid, Tok Mat to remain party's top two
">{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2023/01/14/umno-assembly-gives-the-nod-to-no-contest-motion-zahid-tok-mat-to-remain-party039s-top-two|title=Umno assembly gives the nod to no-contest motion, Zahid, Tok Mat to remain party's top two|work=The Star|accessdate=14 January 2023}}</ref> 

On 28 January 2023, [[Khairy Jamaluddin]] and [[Noh Omar]] was sacked from the party while [[Hishammuddin Hussein]], [[Shahril Sufian Hamdan]], Maulizan Bujang and [[Salim Shariff]] was suspended their membership for six years by the UMNO supreme council.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Umno confirms sacking of Khairy along with Noh, Hishammuddin and Shahril suspended in Zahid's purge |url=https://malaysia.news.yahoo.com/umno-confirms-sacking-khairy-along-161751814.html |access-date=2023-01-27 |website=malaysia.news.yahoo.com |date=27 January 2023 |language=en-MY}}</ref>
