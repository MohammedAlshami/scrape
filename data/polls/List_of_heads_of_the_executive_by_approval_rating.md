This is a list of [[List of current heads of state and government|current heads]] of the [[Executive (government)|executive]] by domestic [[approval rating]]. The highest current total approval percentage is difficult to determine because of the high falsification rate in countries where answering an approval question negatively could lead to government repression. For example, on the basis of a 2020 poll, [[General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party]] [[Xi Jinping]] appeared to have had a 94% approval rate; after controlling for answer falsification, it was revealed to be >73%.<ref name="Bggtt2024">{{cite journal |date=2024-01-10 |first1=Erin |last1=Baggott Carter |first2=Brett L. |last2=Carter |first3=Stephen |last3=Schick |title=Do Chinese Citizens Conceal Opposition to the CCP in Surveys? Evidence from Two Experiments |pages=804–813 |url=https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/china-quarterly/article/do-chinese-citizens-conceal-opposition-to-the-ccp-in-surveys-evidence-from-two-experiments/12A2440F948D016E8D845C492F7D0CFE |doi=10.1017/S0305741023001819 |journal=The China Quarterly |eissn=1468-2648 |volume=259|doi-access=free }} See [https://drive.google.com/file/d/184SwBiJed289nznzLDnAg74ow2Q_4Qwb/view appendix].</ref> The [[rally 'round the flag effect]] comes into play during periods of [[war]] or [[international crisis]]. Genuine high approval ratings can be explained by a number of factors. [[James Stimson]] termed the higher ratings shortly after an election the "honeymoon effect".<ref>{{cite journal |date=January 1976 |first=James A. |last=Stimson |author-link=James Stimson |title=Public Support for American Presidents: A Cyclical Model |pages=1–20 |doi=10.1086/268264 |journal=Public Opinion Quarterly |issn=0033-362X |volume=40 |issue=1}}</ref>

[[Dina Boluarte]] of [[Peru]], prior to her [[Impeachment of Dina Boluarte|2025 impeachment]], was considered "perhaps the most unpopular president in the world", with 2% approval according to Ipsos.<ref>{{Cite web |title=La Nación / Presidenta de Perú registra solo un 2 % de aprobación |url=https://www.lanacion.com.py/mundo/2025/06/01/presidenta-de-peru-registra-solo-un-2-de-aprobacion/ |date=June 1, 2025|access-date=December 14, 2025 |website=lanacion.com.py |language=es}}</ref> Other exceptionally low approval ratings from reliable polls include 2.8% for [[Haiti]]'s [[Ariel Henry]] after taking office following the assassination of [[Jovenel Moïse]] in 2021 and 3% for [[Suriname]]'s [[Chan Santokhi]], both taken by [[Vanderbilt University]]'s ''AmericasBarometer'' project.<ref name="AmB2023-11">{{cite web |date=November 2023 |first1=Noam |last1=Lupu |first2=Liz |last2=Zechmeister |title=LAPOP's AmericaBarometer takes the Pulse of Democracy in Suriname |url=https://www.vanderbilt.edu/lapop/suriname/ABSUR2023-Pulse-of-Democracy-final-20240529.pdf |website=Vanderbilt.edu|publisher=Vanderbilt University |archive-date=June 30, 2024|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240630121419/https://www.vanderbilt.edu/lapop/suriname/ABSUR2023-Pulse-of-Democracy-final-20240529.pdf}}</ref>{{rp|44}}
