'''Malaysia Madani''' ({{langx|en|'''Civilised Malaysia'''}})<ref>{{Cite web |title=MyGOV - Belanjawan 2023 |url=https://www.malaysia.gov.my/portal/category/365 |access-date=3 June 2024 |website=[[Government of Malaysia]]}}</ref> is a [[political slogan]] introduced by the administration of the 10th [[Prime Minister of Malaysia|Prime Minister]], [[Anwar Ibrahim]] to refer to the policy framework of his government.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web |last=BERNAMA |date=2023-03-25 |title=WHAT IS MALAYSIA MADANI? |url=https://www.bernama.com/en/infographics/index.php?v=10698 |access-date=2023-03-24 |website=BERNAMA |language=en}}</ref> The concept focuses on [[good governance]], [[sustainable development]] and [[Demographics of Malaysia|racial harmony]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=CNA Explains: What does Anwar's Malaysia Madani slogan mean and how does it represent the new government? |url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/asia/malaysia-madani-slogan-new-government-anwar-ibrahim-3219941 |access-date=2023-03-24 |website=CNA |language=en}}</ref> 

Malaysia Madani serves as the replacement for [[Keluarga Malaysia]], of the administration of the 9th Prime Minister, [[Ismail Sabri Yaakob]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite news |last=Nor Ain Mohamed Radhi |date=19 January 2023 |title=PM launches Malaysia MADANI slogan |work=New Straits Times |url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2023/01/871706/pm-launches-malaysia-madani-slogan |access-date=25 March 2023}}</ref>

== Overview ==
The Malaysia Madani concept was first published in a book and launched on 2 October 2022 in the Dorsett Grand Hotel in Subang, a month before the [[2022 Malaysian general election|2022 Malaysia general election]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=SCRIPT – For a Better Malaysia by Anwar Ibrahim |url=https://ideselangor.com/v2/2022/10/03/script-for-a-better-malaysia-by-anwar-ibrahim/ |access-date=2023-03-24 |language=en-GB |archive-date=2023-01-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230121035706/https://ideselangor.com/v2/2022/10/03/script-for-a-better-malaysia-by-anwar-ibrahim/ |url-status=dead }}</ref> After Anwar's appointment as the Prime Minister, the Malaysia Madani concept was introduced as a national policy on 19 January 2023 in [[Putrajaya]].<ref name=":0" />

Madani is the acronym for the core values: ke'''M'''ampanan (sustainability), kesej'''A'''hteraan (prosperity), '''D'''aya cipta (innovation), horm'''A'''t (respect), keyaki'''N'''an (trust) and '''I'''hsan (compassion).<ref>{{Cite web |title=Madani – a humane concept |url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2023/01/20/madani---a-humane-concept |access-date=2023-03-24 |website=The Star |language=en}}</ref> The concept mainly targets the following domains:<ref name=":1" />

{{col-begin|width=40%}}
{{Col-4}}
* [[Economy of Malaysia|Economy and finance]]
* Legislation
* Institution 
* Education
{{Col-4}}
* Community
* Culture
* Urban
* Rural
{{col-end}}
