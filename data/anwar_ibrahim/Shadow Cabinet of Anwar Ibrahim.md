On 9 May 2021, the Pakatan Harapan secretariat has announced the members of nine committees to address issues of the day, given the [[Perikatan Nasional]] government's "incompetency" and "inability to lead the country". The committees are Education, Health, Economy, Security, Gender, Youth, Cost-of-Living, Legal, Mobilisation, Plantation and Food Industry, and Communication, Digital and Multimedia. With the Education and Health committee led by [[Simpang Renggam (federal constituency)|Simpang Renggam]] MP [[Maszlee Malik]] and [[Kuala Selangor (federal constituency)|Kuala Selangor]] MP [[Dzulkefly Ahmad]], respectively.<ref>{{Cite news|date=9 May 2021|title=Pakatan names members of 9 panels formed due to govt's 'inability to lead'|work=The Vibes|url=https://www.thevibes.com/articles/news/26765/pakatan-names-members-of-9-panels-formed-due-to-govts-inability-to-lead|access-date=9 May 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Ayub |first=Salahuddin |date=19 September 2021 |title=[KANDUNGAN SIDANG MEDIA] Jawatankuasa... |url=https://www.facebook.com/239023542869472/posts/3995616017210187/ |website=Facebook}}</ref> On 24 November 2022, the shadow cabinet was dissolved following the appointment of Anwar as the 10th Prime Minister and the return of PH to the federal government.
